Introduction

Greetings! Welcome to Visionix’s Handbook. This MR+ handbook has all of the crucial information for our staff members. Please use this as a reference at any time throughout your journey here at Visionix.

General Rules

As one of ROBLOX’s ophthalmologists, our MR+ represents Visionix. We have very high expectations for every one of our MR+ that are employed here. While we expect you to follow all regulations that are posted throughout our information channels and other guides, we have some basic rules that you also must follow which can be found below.

Be Professional

You should be professional at all times while in any of our experiences. This includes being mature, respectful, and organized.

Be Respectful

At no time should any of our MR+s be disrespectful to anyone. You are setting an example for many other members of our group. Being disrespectful will not be tolerated. This includes our Discord server; failure to do so will result in consequences.

Do Not Leak Private Information

Leaking is a major offense, and will result in a ban. Leaking includes giving documents to people who are not an MR+, sharing any messages from a member of Staff Management to anyone outside of Staff Management, etc.

Follow All Rules Given

This means that you follow all rules that are listed above this, as well as any rules that are shared in an announcement, or given by a High-rank+.

Channels

As an MR+, you will be able to access more channels. These channels are **confidential**, meaning that they are only to be discussed with fellow members of management. Absolutely none of our MR+ channels should be leaked, including the names & content within.

Depending on your rank, you may have access to additional channels not included here.

Promotions

Promotions are a very common way we reward individuals who exceed and perform exceptionally. If you are interested in advancing within our rank structure, you must continuously display a high level of effort and dedication which will make you stand out and get noticed.

  • Display a consistent level of activity within the game.
  • Excel within your weekly assignments.
  • Host or attend **trainings & sessions**.
  • Engage and be **active in the community chat**.
  • Have a clean moderation and punishment history.

Please remember that hinting or asking for a promotion will only reduce your chances.

Punishment Handbook

Standard Protocol

Our protocol is to: Remind (once) > Warn (once) > Kick > Warn (once) > Ban

Punishments: MRs (Rank 9-11+)

1. Trolling: Remind > Warn > Kick

E.g: "i wanna order mcdonalds"

2. Inappropriate/Disrespectful Chat: Warn > Kick

E.g: "i hate you fatsoo"

3. Inappropriate Avatar: Remind > Kick

E.g: Big avatars that can distract and eye seeing.

Punishments: HRs (Rank 12-14+)

1. Flooding Chat: Kick > Ban

E.g: Spamming/flooding the same word in the chat.

2. Dating/Suicidal Topic: Kick > Ban

E.g: "i love you beybe" / "di3 for me"

3. ALTs (Alternative Accounts/Clones): Ban > Request Pban

Note: Make sure to get proof before banning/pban!

4. Glitching through walls: Respawn > Remind > Ban / Req Pban

E.g: Like using an emote to glitch through walls are not allowed.

5. Inappropriate Emote: Remind > Ban

Note: If you think it's inappropriate, make sure to capture evidence before banning!

6. Exploiting: Ban / Req Pban

E.g: Flying around using hacks/exploits.

Reminder for HRs+:

Please keep in mind that you will have the ability to **ban**. Make sure to have **evidence** before banning someone. Failure to do so may result in termination.

Punishments: Corporate Team (Rank 15+)

1. Hating the Game: Pban

E.g: Hating the game is not allowed, results in permanent ban. Must capture evidence first before banning it.

2. Personal Info Leak: Pban

E.g: Someone leaking their own personal info like address, etc. It’s not allowed!

3. Leaking Info/Handbook: Terminated > Pban

E.g: Leaking our information/handbook is not allowed and can be terminated and appealed. It depends on the situation.

4. Unprofessional Staff: Remind > Warn > Demoted

Reminder for Corp Team:

Please keep in mind that you will have the ability to **pban**. Make sure to have **evidence** before p-banning someone. Failure to do so may result in termination.

Moderation Commands

Unlike the MR+, management can use moderation commands at Visionix. These commands should be used appropriately. **Admin abuse is not tolerated** and will result in severe punishment. Always include a reason when using a command!

Command Description Permission / Notes
:bans Shows a list of server bans. ALLOWED
:pm Sends a personal message. ALLOWED
:to Teleport to a player. ALLOWED
:info Get information about a player. ALLOWED
:joinlogs Log of all joins. ALLOWED
:ban Bans player from server. ALLOWED **only if** the player followed the full `kick > warn > ban` protocol.
:respawn / :res Respawns player. ALLOWED (must include a reason).
:kick Kicks player from server. ALLOWED **only if** the player followed the `remind > warn` protocol.
:unban Unbans player from server. ALLOWED **only if** the user appeals or Corp Team approves.
:cmds Shows command list. ALLOWED
:admins / :ingameadmins Shows admins in-game. ALLOWED
:chatlogs Logs of all chats. ALLOWED
:view / :unview Views/unviews player. ALLOWED
:refresh / :res Refreshes player. ALLOWED (give valid reason).
:ff / :unff Adds a forcefield to player. ALLOWED **IF AFK**
:speed / :ws Increases/decreases speed. NOT ALLOWED
:m Announces a message. ONLY FOR CORPS
:team Changes a player’s team. NOT ALLOWED
:shutdown Shutdown the server. NOT ALLOWED (only allowed if hosting a training and only the host/overseer can do it).

Conclusion: use commands with high responsibility
